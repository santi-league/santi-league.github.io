📊Rating Calculation Formula

▼

Main Formula

Rating Change = Games Correction × (Point Change + Rating Correction)
① Point Change (in thousands)
Score Diff / 1000 + Uma Points
Uma Points:
• M-League: 1st=+45, 2nd=+5, 3rd=-15, 4th=-35
• EMA: 1st=+15, 2nd=+5, 3rd=-5, 4th=-15
Score Diff: Final Score - Starting Score
(M-League starts at 25,000, EMA starts at 30,000)
② Rating Correction
(Table Avg Rating - Your Rating) / 40
• Stronger opponents: positive correction
• Weaker opponents: negative correction
→ Stronger players lose less, win more; weaker players lose more, win less
③ Games Correction
Games < 400: 1 - Games × 0.002
Games ≥ 400: 0.2
• Early games: High Rating volatility
• Later games: Stable Rating (fixed at 0.2)
💡 Calculation Example
Scenario: 1st place, final score 38,000, current Rating=1650, table avg Rating=1600, 50 games played
Calculation:
• Point Change = 13000 / 1000 + 45 = 58
• Rating Correction = (1600 - 1650) / 40 = -1.25
• Games Correction = 1 - 50 × 0.002 = 0.9
• Rating Change = 0.9 × (58 - 1.25) = +51.08
→ New Rating: 1701.08
